About us.

We are two Senior High School students from Bulacan State University who over the course of four years have formed an impeccable friendship.  Throughout the time, we learned that we both share a passion for clothing  and have been each other's guide for styling and fitting outfits. We took this as an inspiration for our blog: Retrovine.

Purpose.


Retrovine aspires to give outfit inspirations and tips on how to properly style pieces. Along with this idea, we also want to promote thrifting, which means to buy second hand clothes or just clothes in general with low prices. There goes a saying: History repeats itself and we believe that Fashion is too. Currently, the 90's are the trending and thrifting would be a good way to find outfits from that decade. Aside from this notion, with the ongoing climate change, reusing clothes would help as it  is basically recycling.
