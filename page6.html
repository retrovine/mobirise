Monica Geller Mini Lookbook.

While binge watching F.R.I.E.N.D.S on netflix, I can't help but be awed with one of it's main cast's looks. This is my attempt on recreating Monica Geller's outfits with the clothes available at my home.

Look #1.

Previous Next

Mom Jeans are back in style and it is not hard to see why. It is comfortable to wear and will go almost with anything. Because this jeans is high waisted, it can accentuate your curves and give off an illusion that you're tall(kind of). Here, I partnered it with a red crop top, black belt and my continental shoes.  To further spice up the look, I put on denim jacket that made the outfit a whole lot cuter. 

Tip: Don't be afraid to layer clothes! May it be denim or leather jacket, or any other jacket, it may help to make an outfit pop up. Experiment layering!

A monochrome look can never go wrong. With shades ranging from black to white, it makes it easy on the eyes to look at. This look consists of: a black ribbed top, a checkered trousers and my trusty continental shoes.  

Tip: Invest in a sturdy white shoes, because it goes with everything. Whether its branded or not, I guarantee that it will work.
